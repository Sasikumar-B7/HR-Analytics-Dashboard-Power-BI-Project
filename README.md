# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze workforce data and uncover key insights related to employee attrition, demographics, and job satisfaction. The dashboard helps HR teams and business stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Requirements

The objective of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall workforce metrics and attrition trends
* Identify key factors influencing employee attrition
* Analyze employee distribution across departments, age groups, and education fields
* Evaluate job satisfaction across different roles
* Provide actionable insights for HR decision-making

## 📈 Key Insights

### 👥 Workforce Overview

* Total Employees: **1470**
* Attrition: **237 employees**
* Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
* Active Employees: **1233**
* Average Age: **37 years**

---

### 🏢 Department-wise Attrition

* **R&D department** has the highest attrition (~56%), indicating potential retention issues
* **Sales** follows with ~39% attrition
* **HR** has the lowest attrition (~5%), showing better employee stability

---

### 🎂 Age Group Analysis

* Majority of employees fall in the **25–34 and 35–44 age groups**
* Attrition is more prominent among **younger employees (Under 35)**
* Senior age groups show comparatively lower attrition

---

### 😊 Job Satisfaction Insights

* Most employees fall under **rating 3 and 4**, indicating moderate to high satisfaction
* Roles like **Sales Executive and Research Scientist** have higher employee counts and varied satisfaction levels
* Some roles show lower satisfaction distribution, indicating scope for HR intervention

---

### 🎓 Education Field-wise Attrition

* Highest attrition observed in:

  * **Life Sciences**
  * **Medical**
* Lower attrition in:

  * **Human Resources**
  * **Other fields**

---

### 🚻 Attrition by Gender & Age Group

* Attrition is generally higher among **male employees across most age groups**
* In younger age groups (Under 35), attrition is significantly higher
* Gender gap reduces slightly in higher age groups

---

## 💡 Conclusion

This dashboard highlights critical areas such as high attrition in specific departments and younger workforce segments. It enables HR teams to:

* Focus on retention strategies in R&D and Sales
* Improve engagement for younger employees
* Address role-specific job satisfaction issues
* Align hiring and retention strategies with education trends
